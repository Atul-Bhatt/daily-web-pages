Delhi

Delhi,India's capital territory,is a massive metropolitan area in the country's north.In Old Delhi , a neighbourhood dating to the 1600s, stands the imposing Mughal-era Red Fort, a symbol of India,and the sprawling Jama Masjid mosque, whose courtyard accomodates 25,000 people. Nearby is Chandini Chowk, a vibratn bazaar filled with food carts, sweet shops and spice stalls.

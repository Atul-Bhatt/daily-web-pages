Uttarakhand, a state in northern India crossed by the Himalyas, is knoen for its Hindu pilgrimage sites. Rishikesh, a major center for yoga study, was made famous by the Beatles' 1968 visit. The city hoststhe evening Ganga Aarti, a spiritual gathering on the sacred Ganges River. The state's forested Jim corbett National Park shelters Bengal Tigers and other native wildlife.
